Ayogram — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025 | Developer: Mohammad Saif | Contact: ayogram@aayoapp.com

Ayogram ("we," "our," or "us") values your trust and is fully committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect data when you use our mobile application (Ayogram), accessible worldwide. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To deliver a secure and personalized messaging experience, Ayogram collects the following categories of information:

  • Personal Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to create and manage your account.
  • Communication Data: Includes your chats, group messages, channels, and media (photos, videos, audio files) shared through the app.
  • Device & Technical Data: Device model, OS version, IP address, Firebase Instance ID, crash logs, and diagnostics for improving performance and detecting abuse.
  • Permissions Data: Camera, microphone, notifications, and storage access are requested to support chat functionality and media sharing.
  • Ad Interaction Data: When using AdMob or Unity Ads, limited anonymous data (such as device ID or region) may be collected for ad delivery and frequency capping.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide, improve, and secure Ayogram’s services:

  • Authenticate your account and manage your access through Google Login.
  • Deliver and store messages, media, and channels using Firebase and Cloudflare services.
  • Display relevant ads through AdMob and Unity while respecting your consent settings.
  • Prevent spam, fraud, or abuse using analytics and security measures.
  • Monitor performance, analyze crashes, and optimize infrastructure.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect user safety.

3. Data Storage and Infrastructure

We use secure, globally recognized infrastructure providers to store user data:

  • Firebase: Used for real-time messaging, authentication, and account data storage.
  • Cloudflare: Used to store and deliver multimedia files (photos, videos, audio) and improve network performance globally.

All communications between your device and our servers are encrypted using modern protocols (HTTPS/TLS). Data is stored securely with restricted access and regular monitoring to prevent unauthorized entry or misuse.

4. Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR Compliance)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Ayogram processes data lawfully based on:

  • Consent: When you create an account or agree to use the app’s features.
  • Legitimate Interest: To maintain service security, performance, and updates.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with regulatory or law enforcement requirements.

5. Advertisement and Monetization

Ayogram displays ads using third-party networks such as Google AdMob and Unity Ads. These networks may use anonymized identifiers or cookies to personalize ad delivery. Users can manage ad personalization directly through Google’s Ad Settings or their device privacy settings.

We ensure all advertising partners comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations. We do not allow adult, gambling, or misleading content.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or trade your personal information. Data is shared only in the following limited cases:

  • With infrastructure partners (Firebase, Cloudflare, AdMob, Unity) strictly to enable core app functions.
  • With legal authorities when required to comply with applicable law, regulation, or court order.
  • In case of a merger, acquisition, or reorganization, with notice to affected users.

7. Data Retention Policy

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Chat messages and media remain stored while your account is active.
  • Upon deletion, personal and message data are permanently erased within 30 days.
  • Backup data is automatically purged after 60 days unless legally required to retain.

8. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

As a global user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or outdated data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data under specific circumstances.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of data collection for ads or analytics anytime.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ayogram@aayoapp.com.

9. Children’s Privacy

Ayogram is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

10. Data Security

We use strong encryption, secure APIs, and limited data access protocols to protect your information. All employees and developers are bound by confidentiality agreements. In the event of a data breach, users will be notified in compliance with GDPR and other applicable laws.

11. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Because Ayogram operates globally, your data may be processed on servers located in different countries. We ensure that such transfers comply with international data protection standards through approved frameworks and safeguards.

12. Compliance with Laws

Ayogram complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – EU)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA – USA)
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)

13. Enforcement and Violation

Users must use Ayogram responsibly. Any misuse, including spam, harassment, or illegal activity, may result in suspension or permanent deletion of your account under this policy.

14. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect service improvements or legal updates. The latest version will always be available in the App and on our website. Continued use of Ayogram after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
